Effective Communication Strategies

This course provides a comprehensive overview of effective communication strategies, focusing on both verbal and non-verbal techniques. Participants will learn how to articulate their thoughts clearly, actively listen to others, and adapt their communication style to different audiences and situations. The course also covers conflict resolution and strategies for building rapport and trust in professional settings.
